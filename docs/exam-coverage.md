# Exam coverage

## CAS Exam 6U (US)

**Status:** In development

Loci's first exam track covers the full **CAS Exam 6U** syllabus — Regulation and Financial Reporting (United States).

### Syllabus domains

| Domain | Topic | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| **A** | United States Laws and Regulations | 10–20% |
| **B** | Government Programs | 5–15% |
| **C** | Financial Reporting and Professional Responsibilities of the Actuary | 60–75% |
| **D** | Reinsurance Accounting Principles | 5–10% |

### What you get

- One **estate** for the full 6U syllabus
- A **2D estate map** to navigate domains and track progress
- **Task rooms** per domain with illustrated scenes, hotspots, puzzles, and reference readings
- **Mnemonic gallery** and document library tied to syllabus concepts

Domain C (financial reporting) is the flagship wing in early releases; other domains follow the same room model.
